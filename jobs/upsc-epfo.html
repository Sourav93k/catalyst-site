UPSC EPFO EO / AO Recruitment 2025 Notification

The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has released the official notification for Enforcement Officer (EO) / Accounts Officer (AO) positions under the Employees' Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) for the year 2025.

Age Limit & Eligibility (as of 18/08/2025)

Selection Process

The selection process consists of a Recruitment Test (RT) followed by an Interview, with specific weightage assigned:

  1. Recruitment Test (RT) - Written Exam
  2. Interview
  3. Document Verification
  4. Medical Examination

Weightage: RT (75%) and Interview (25%).

Recruitment Test (RT) Pattern

The RT is an objective type written exam with 100 questions. Key details:

  • Total Marks: 300 (3 marks per question)
  • Time Duration: 2 Hours (120 minutes)
  • Negative Marking: 1/3rd mark for each wrong answer.
